Top Surgery (FTM Chest Masculinization)

VJ Transgender Clinics

Customized Surgical Techniques
Top surgery is not one-size-fits-all. The choice between Double Incision, Inverted T, or Buttonhole techniques depends on your chest size, skin elasticity, and personal goals.

Permanent Gender-Affirming Results
The procedure offers long-lasting, irreversible masculinization of the chest, helping reduce gender dysphoria and aligning your physical appearance with your gender identity.

Nipple Grafting May Be Required
In larger chest sizes, the nipples are typically removed and grafted in a more masculine position, ensuring symmetry and natural appearance post-surgery.

Healing Takes Time and Support
Most patients resume light activities within a week, but full recovery and final results may take several months. Wearing a compression garment and following post-op care is essential.

Surgical Process:

Initial Consultation & Evaluation
A thorough assessment of your medical history, chest size, skin elasticity, and desired outcomes. Photographs and physical exam to determine the most suitable surgical technique.

Pre-Surgical Preparation
Blood tests, health evaluations, and hormone therapy review (if applicable). Pre-op instructions include stopping certain medications and fasting guidelines.

Surgical Procedure
Technique Selection: Double Incision with free nipple grafting (common for larger chests), Peri-Areolar, or Keyhole (for smaller chests).

Post-Surgery Care & Recovery
A compression garment is worn to reduce swelling and promote proper healing. Drains may be placed temporarily to remove fluid build-up. Stitches typically dissolve or are removed in follow-up visits.

TRANSGENDER SURGERY COST

Female to Male (FTM) surgery involves multiple procedures and the cost may vary based on the specific surgeries selected. It's a life-changing step that requires expert guidance and informed decisions.

The cost of FTM gender-affirming surgery in India typically ranges from ₹4,00,000 to ₹8,00,000 depending on the procedures such as top surgery, phalloplasty, and hysterectomy.

Genital reconstruction surgeries like metoidioplasty or phalloplasty require multiple stages and can include urethral lengthening, scrotoplasty, and testicular implants.

Facial masculinization and body contouring procedures, such as jaw reshaping and liposuction, may also contribute to the total cost and are customized per individual goals.

FTM Top Surgery is one of the most commonly chosen gender-affirming procedures for trans men worldwide.

Metoidioplasty allows many trans men to urinate while standing by extending the hormonally enlarged clitoris.

Phalloplasty can include penile implants to enable erectile function and sexual activity.

Scrotoplasty can be performed with testicular implants to create a natural-looking scrotum.
