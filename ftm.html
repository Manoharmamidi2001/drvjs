TRANSGENDER SURGERY COST

Female to Male (FTM) surgery involves multiple procedures and the cost may vary based on the specific surgeries selected. It's a life-changing step that requires expert guidance and informed decisions.

The cost of FTM gender-affirming surgery in India typically ranges from ₹4,00,000 to ₹8,00,000 depending on the procedures such as top surgery, phalloplasty, and hysterectomy.

Genital reconstruction surgeries like metoidioplasty or phalloplasty require multiple stages and can include urethral lengthening, scrotoplasty, and testicular implants.

Facial masculinization and body contouring procedures, such as jaw reshaping and liposuction, may also contribute to the total cost and are customized per individual goals.
