This surgery could be a vital transition for your life, which is why there are some things that you need to be aware of before the procedure:-

  • Like every other surgery, there are some potential risks and complications that you could experience after the top surgery. So, it will be beneficial for you to carefully consider if you want to go through this surgery or not.
  • If you have a habit of smoking and you are unable to quit that habit, then you should reconsider the decision of undergoing this surgery because smoking severely affects this procedure and you should quit smoking for at least two months before the surgery.
  • There are some medications that you should stop taking, like ibuprofen and aspirin, because if you have consumed these medicines before the surgery. Then, a high risk of bleeding develops during or after the procedure.
  • You have to maintain good physical health, which will help you in a quick recovery from the chest masculinisation surgery. By eating a healthy diet, exercising you can enhance your physical health.

You need to keep in mind that this procedure is complex and has a longer recovery period. This is why you need to make recovery preparations before the Top surgery:-

  • Taking a break from work is essential, as it will help you get properly healed. You should get a break for at least 2 to 4 weeks after the surgery.
  • You should hire a driver or a person that you can rely on so that they can drive you to the medical facilities for follow-up appointments and checkups during the recovery phase.
  • Any family member, friend or professional caregiver should be present so that you can get the much-needed assistance in completing the daily activities like bathing, brushing or even dressing.
  • You should have ample stock of supplies like medicines, bandages, gauze and loose clothes. The loose clothing will keep you comfortable during the recovery period.

After the Top surgery in Bengaluru, these are some recommendations for you if you want a quick and healthy recovery:-

  • If you have undergone Top surgery, then you should avoid taking a shower for some time. The alternate way of maintaining healthy hygiene is by using sponge baths or wipes.
  • It is important to avoid any exercise that requires lifting heavy stuff. If you are beginning to feel better, then you could add some light-intensity exercises to your schedule.
  • Experiencing pain and discomfort is normal, and to counter these effects, you should follow the prescribed medication or apply ice packs to the area of pain.
  • You have to keep your body hydrated by drinking plenty of water because it can reduce the risk of constipation, which is another side effect of eating the medications for pain.
  • If you want to boost your healing process and decrease the redness and scar tissues on your body, then we will advise you to undergo over-the-counter scar treatments.
