Anesthesia & Preparation
The patient is placed under general anesthesia. The genital area is sterilized and prepped for surgery.

Removal & Reconstruction
The penis and testes are removed, but important nerves and skin are preserved. The penile skin is inverted to create a neovaginal canal.

Creation of Neovagina & Neoclitoris
A space is created between the rectum and bladder to house the neovagina. The glans penis (rich in nerve endings) is reshaped into a neoclitoris to retain sexual sensation.
